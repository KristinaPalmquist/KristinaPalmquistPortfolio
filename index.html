About

I am a system developer based in Stockholm with a passion for both backend and frontend programming. Before discovering my love for coding, I worked in finance, focusing on AR (accounts receivable). Recently, I completed a two-year JavaScript Developer program at IT-Högskolan. During that time, I also earned my bachelor’s degree in Business Administration from Stockholm University and took additional university courses in IT. I am currently enrolled in a six-month System Developer Python and AI program at Lexicon AB, which will be followed by a 12-week internship.

I enjoy tackling challenges, exploring new technologies, and turning ideas into reality through code. Whether I’m designing user interfaces or implementing someone else’s design, or working with databases, I love diving into the details and finding creative solutions.

Garn & Stickor

This is the result of an assignment in the course "Web Technology 1" at Linneaus University. The task was to create a website for a fictional store or similar.

More GitHub

I chose to make a website for a fictional yarn shop called Garn & Stickat. The purpose of the site was to inspire visitors and help both new and returning customers find the shop. The website is not an online store, but aims to attract new customers—especially those who are curious about knitting or handcrafts but may not have tried it before.

User needs addressed:

  • See that the shop exists
  • Find directions to the shop
  • Get an overview of the shop’s product range
  • Find inspiration for handcrafts, especially knitting
